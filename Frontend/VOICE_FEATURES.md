# Voice Banking Assistant

## ChatGPT-Style Voice Interface

Your banking assistant now features a ChatGPT-style voice bot interface that makes banking interactions feel natural and conversational.

### How to Use

1. **Launch Voice Chat**: Click the "Voice Chat" button in the header of the dashboard
2. **Start Speaking**: Click the large microphone button in the center to begin
3. **Speak Naturally**: Say your banking query naturally - the assistant will listen and respond
4. **Continue Conversation**: Simply click the mic again to ask follow-up questions

### Key Features

**Automatic Voice Processing**
- Speak directly into the microphone
- Your voice is automatically converted to text and sent to the backend
- No need to manually submit - it happens automatically when you stop speaking

**Voice Responses**
- The assistant speaks back your responses using natural speech synthesis
- Toggle voice on/off using the volume icon in the header
- Click "Stop" button to interrupt speaking

**Clean, Minimal Interface**
- Full-screen conversation view like ChatGPT
- Clear message bubbles for user and assistant
- Real-time transcription feedback while listening
- Intuitive status messages

### Example Voice Commands

- "What's my account balance?"
- "Show me recent transactions"
- "I want to pay John Smith fifty dollars"
- "How much have I spent this month?"
- "List my saved payees"

### Browser Compatibility

**Supported Browsers**:
- Chrome (Desktop & Mobile)
- Edge
- Safari (iOS & Mac)
- Opera

**Not Supported**:
- Firefox (use text chat instead)
- Older browser versions

### Technical Details

The voice interface uses standard Web APIs:
- **Web Speech API** - For voice recognition and synthesis
- **No external dependencies** - Everything runs locally in your browser
- **Privacy-focused** - Only your transcribed text is sent to the backend

### Tips for Best Results

1. Use a quiet environment for better voice recognition
2. Speak clearly and at a normal pace
3. Allow the assistant to finish speaking before speaking again
4. Check your browser's microphone permissions if voice isn't working
